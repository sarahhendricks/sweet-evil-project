Blackberry Vanilla Cake

Simple, but oh so sweet! Pillowy vanilla cake topped with clouds of vanilla frosting, ringed by only the season's best blackberries. A summer treat perfect for any picnic!

Macaroon Cake

A sugary indulgence perfect for a queen! A colorful sprinkle cake frosted with a hint of vanilla, topped with a dozen of our handmade macaroons. Enjoy a slice and daydream of Paris!

Devil's Chocolate Cake

You can't be a queen without a dark side, and this cake fits the bill! A rich chocolate fudge cake topped with midnight chocolate frosting. Perfect with a scoop of ice cream!

Oreo Cake

The light and the dark, the crunchy and the soft -- you can have it all with this cake! A vanilla cake mixed with oreo crumbs and layered with oreo dust frosting, topped with fudge. Now you can have both your cookie AND cake, and eat them too!

Tiramisu Cake

A classy cake for a classy lady. Traditional ladyfinger cookies soaked in espresso, layered with mascapone cream and chocolate. Pack your bags for this one -- you'll be transported to Italy in a heartbeat!

Strawberry Cake

Perfect for spring, this cake is as sweet as it gets! Vanilla pound cake and fluffy vanilla cream frosting, topped with the most lucious strawberries of the season. Sure to leave you daydreaming about sunny skies and open waters!
